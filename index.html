About Me

I'm a 21 year old Information Science Engineering undergraduate based in Bengaluru, India. I have always had a predilection towards technology and innovation. I aspire to use cutting edge tech for problem solving, which got me into learning and working on artificial intelligence. My other areas of interest include Internet of Things, distributed computing, blockchain and economics. I firmly believe in the power of community and love meeting people from different backgrounds as well working with them. I've trained numerous students, faculty and professionals in machine learning and IoT across India via hands-on workshops and online classes.
In addition, I'm a former musician- professionaly trained in Tabla and bit of classical music (can play the harmonium as well :P), ardent cricketer too! I spend my free time watching movies and web series, catching up with family and friends. I'm always on a lookout for food and an opportunity to pack my bags for travel escapade!
